Feature Hub

Everything people can actually do with Codunot.

Core AI Chat

  • Talk in servers using @Codunot your message.
  • In DMs, just type normally.
  • !codunot_help opens quick command help.

Mode System (both prefix + slash)

  • !funmode or /funmode
  • !roastmode or /roastmode
  • !seriousmode or /seriousmode
  • !teachmerizz online|irl or /teachmerizz
  • !chessmode or /chessmode

Creative + Utility Tools

  • Image generation
  • Video generation
  • Text-to-speech
  • Image editing
  • Image merging (send 2+ images and include one of the MERGE_KEYWORDS in your message)
  • Video transcription from YouTube/Twitch/X/Kick links
  • File reading and summaries

Fun Commands

  • /hug, /kiss, /kick, /slap
  • /wish_goodmorning for greetings
  • /meme for random memes
  • /bet heads or /bet tails for quick games
Most advanced tools are vote-unlocked for 12 hours after each top.gg vote.
