Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use Codunot in servers?

Mention @Codunot before your message in server channels.

Does prefix + slash both work for modes?

Yes. Example: !funmode and /funmode both work.

How does image merging work?

Send 2+ images and include one of the merge phrases from MERGE_KEYWORDS in your message.

What does /transcribe do?

It extracts text from supported video links like YouTube, Twitch, X and Kick.

What is app login?

The Login button authorizes Codunot as a Discord app flow using the app link.
